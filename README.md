# StoryThreader Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

## 1. Overview
StoryThreader (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides tools to generate social content drafts.  
This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed when you use the app.

## 2. Data We Process

### 2.1 User Content
When you generate content, the text you enter (“Input”) and the generated output may be stored locally on your device as part of your history.

### 2.2 API Keys (BYOK)
If you choose a Bring Your Own Key (BYOK) provider (for example OpenAI or Anthropic), your API keys are stored in Apple Keychain on your device.

### 2.3 Subscription Data
If you purchase Premium, subscription and entitlement status is handled by Apple via StoreKit. We do not receive your full payment card data.

### 2.4 Analytics and Diagnostics
The app may record limited product usage events (for example generate taps, paywall views, purchase/restore outcomes, and experiment variants) to improve app performance and conversion.  
These events are designed to avoid direct personal identification.

## 3. How Data Is Used
We use processed data to:
- generate requested content,
- provide history functionality,
- enable premium features and restore purchases,
- improve output quality and app experience.

## 4. Third-Party Services
Depending on your provider selection, your input may be sent to third-party AI providers you choose (such as OpenAI or Anthropic) using your own API key.  
Their use of your data is governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

Apple may process purchase and subscription information under Apple’s own policies.

## 5. Data Storage
- History data: stored locally on device.
- API keys: stored in Keychain on device.
- We do not claim to sell personal data.

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion
You can delete generated history inside the app.  
You can remove BYOK keys inside the app by clearing the key fields.  
Deleting the app may remove local app data, subject to iOS behavior.

## 7. Children’s Privacy
StoryThreader is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 8. Your Rights
Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data.  
Because most data is stored locally, many actions can be completed directly within the app.

## 9. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will be revised when changes are made.
